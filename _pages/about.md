## Hello! 👋

I'm **Mihir Gupte** (मिहिर गुप्ते), a Software Engineer and AI Researcher currently based in **Royal Oak, Michigan**.

## Current Work

I'm working at **General Motors (GM)** as a Software Engineer / AI Researcher, focusing on the intersection of software engineering and artificial intelligence.

## Research Interests

My research explores several key areas in AI and machine learning:

- **Large Language Models (LLMs)** - Understanding and improving LLM capabilities
- **Context Window Optimization** - Addressing challenges like the "lost-in-the-middle" phenomenon
- **Formal Verification & Autoformalization** - Using AI to formalize mathematical and logical proofs
- **Information Retrieval** - Extracting and processing information from long-context documents
- **AI Methodology** - Developing benchmarks and evaluation frameworks for AI systems

## Recent Work

### Papers

My recent research publications focus on understanding limitations and improvements of large language models:

- **Lost-in-the-Middle Phenomenon** - Studying why LLMs struggle with information in the middle of long contexts
- **Implicit Knowledge in LLMs** - Exploring how language models store and retrieve implicit knowledge
- **Autoformalization** - Investigating AI-assisted formal verification and proof generation
